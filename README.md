## 🔧 Using mini-motion

mini-motion lets you automate video creation with just a few clicks. Here is a simple guide to use the app:

1. Open mini-motion on your computer.

2. To create a new video, start by writing a short script or prompt. The app will help generate video scripts if you need ideas.

3. Choose or create your video background using built-in tools.

4. Add AI-generated voiceovers by typing what you want the video to say. The app will convert the text to speech.

5. Pick background music from the available options or let the app create one for you.

6. Preview your video to check how it looks and sounds.

7. When satisfied, export the final video in an easy-to-share format like MP4.

8. Save your project if you want to edit it later.
